# Drafts

Half-formed thinking, work-in-progress documents, and explorations that aren't ready to be considered "current."

A document lives here when:

- It's an exploration of an idea we may or may not pursue.
- It's a partial thought that needs more research before it makes sense as a `current/` doc.
- It's a working response to a specific conversation that may or may not generalize.

A document graduates from `drafts/` to `current/` when it's stable enough to be treated as the working hypothesis on its topic. Whoever moves it should also write a `decisions/` entry explaining the reasoning.

Read with skepticism. Anything here is more likely to be wrong than anything in `current/`.
